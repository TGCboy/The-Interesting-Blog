Weber's Law

Imagine I told you to I was going to give you £20, but I actually gave you £5 - you'd be pretty annoyed right? Now imagine I told you I was going to give you £1015 and I gave you £1000. You'd barely notice, wouldn't you? Interestingly here the difference in money is £15 both times yet, one scenario leaves you significanly more grumpy than the other. Well believe it or not there is a good reason for this and we called it Weber's Law.
